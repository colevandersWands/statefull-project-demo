Charecterize

The point of this game is to try guessing which characters are in a sentence.
Type anything you want into the 'input' field.
In the 'guess' field type what characters you think this sentence contains without duplication, in charcode order. (spaces and punctuation count)

a couple examples:
- "trolly problem" --> " belmoprty"
- "colon: semi;"" --> " :;ceilmnos"

you can also:
- review your game history by clicking the 'game history' button
- revisit old saved challenges by clicking the 'display challenges' button
- save your current challenge to the history by clicking 'save challenge'
